For You, Shay

I just want to start by saying thank you for everything.

Our conversation jana… it really meant a lot to me. Just knowing that you care the way you do, it honestly means more than I can put into words.

I need you to know this… I love you. And I really don’t want to lose you. You mean too much to me.

I’m sorry… for turning your life upside down, for the confusion, for everything. Truly, I am.

I know I’m not the best at opening up. I know I can be complicated… even messed up sometimes. And sometimes it doesn’t even feel like I’m worthy of your love.

But with you… I feel free. I feel unjudged. And that’s something I don’t get anywhere else.

With you, the pain fades. Things don’t feel as heavy anymore. You make everything feel lighter… better.

I know it might be selfish that I want you all to myself… but it’s only because of how much you mean to me.

I want to make you happy. I want to show you love the way you deserve. I want to be there for you… and I’m willing to put in the work, no matter what it takes.

Because I love you. I really do.

— yours, always
